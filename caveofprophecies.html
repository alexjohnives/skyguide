(Winged Light x 4)

Due to the addition of the Cave of Prophecies and the Trials during theÂ Season of Prophecy, players will receive one Winged Light for each trial they complete. As there are four Trials, there are four Winged Light.

WL 6: Trial of Water

You receive this Winged Light at the end of the Trial of Water.

It stands at the top of the glowing mountain at the end of the Trial.

WL 7: Trial of Earth

You receive this Winged Light at the end of the Trial of Earth.

To reach it, you must complete the maze-like Trial and get to the shining tower at the end of the Trial.

The Child of Light stands in the tower.

WL 8: Trial of Air

You receive this Winged Light at the end of the Trial of Air.

You must climb the towering islands to reach the main island at the top, where the Child of Light stands.

WL 9: Trial of Fire

You receive this Winged Light at the end of the Trial of Fire.

The Child of Light stands after the final checkpoint, after you kill all the monsters.
