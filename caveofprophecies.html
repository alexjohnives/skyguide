There are four trials for players to complete in the Cave of Prophecies.

With each trial completion, you are rewarded one winged light.

WL 6: Trial of Water

You receive this Winged Light at the end of the Trial of Water.

It stands at the top of the glowing mountain at the end of the Trial.

WL 7: Trial of Earth

You receive this Winged Light at the end of the Trial of Earth.

To reach it, you must complete the maze-like Trial and get to the shining tower at the end of the Trial.

The Child of Light stands in the tower.

WL 8: Trial of Air

You receive this Winged Light at the end of the Trial of Air.

You must climb the towering islands to reach the main island at the top where the Child of Light awaits.

WL 9: Trial of Fire

You receive this Winged Light at the end of the Trial of Fire.

The Child of Light stands after the final checkpoint, after the dark creatures vanish.
